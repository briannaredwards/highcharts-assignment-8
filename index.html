How Americans Spend Their Time

By Brianna Edwards

American adults have a wide variety of choices for how to spend their time. The 24-hour day is often broken up into sleep, work and leisure. Every year, the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics takes surveys to evaluate how Americans spend their time and document the changes year to year. The 2018 survey found that American adults spend, on average, 8.82 hours sleeping. View the chart below to see how they spent their time while awake.
